The Motion Inside Every Conversation

Two gates, then the ask.

First gate
Overcome the status quo
"Why change at all?" Make the cost of staying concrete — patients leaking to telehealth, the $150K oversight bill — until doing nothing is the risky choice.
Second gate
Overcome indecision
"Why now, why you?" Remove the risk — 14 days free, month-to-month, compliance handled — so the safe move becomes saying yes.
Then — and only then
Earn the partnership
The ask is the natural close of two problems already solved together — not a push at the end of a pitch.
Ask for the business
Both gates are trust work. Skip either and you get a "yes" that churns — which is why the motion lives upstream of the close, not at it.

The Q4 Tension

3× spend ≠ 3× closes.

The trap

More reps, more speed, more dials. Close rate slides, the consultative sale collapses into a transaction — and trust is spent to hit a number.

The move · Tier the surge

High-fit / high-intent leads get the full consultative motion. A lighter, async-assisted fast-lane works the rest — so nothing the founders paid 3× for goes dark, without diluting the core sale.
